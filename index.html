Blade Runner 2049 is a science fiction film directed by Denis Villeneuve, serving as a sequel to Ridley Scott's 1982 cult classic, Blade Runner. Set thirty years after the events of the original film, Blade Runner 2049 follows a new blade runner named Officer K, who uncovers a long-buried secret that has the potential to plunge what remains of society into chaos.
